About the Studio

One-of-a-kind porcelain pieces for everyday beauty.

Shuli Handcraft Studio is a personal ceramic and handcraft studio focused on small-batch porcelain pieces. Each item is individually painted, shaped, or finished by hand, carrying subtle textures, soft colours, and a quiet handmade warmth.

Rather than mass-produced decoration, these pieces are made to become small, meaningful details in daily life.

Our Story

Made with patience, detail, and heart.

Each porcelain piece begins as a simple object, then slowly becomes something more personal through hand-painted details, soft colours, and delicate surface textures.

The studio values slowness, uniqueness, and the quiet beauty of handmade objects. Every piece may carry slight variations, making it truly one of a kind.
