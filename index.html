About

Small objects with quiet details.

Shuli Studio is a personal handcraft studio focusing on handcrafted porcelain and delicate miniature creations. Each piece is made in small quantities, carrying traces of handwork, time, and careful observation.

The studio celebrates slow making, soft colours, floral details, and objects that bring a gentle sense of beauty into daily life.

Story

Handmade in Jingdezhen, the ceramic capital of China.

Each porcelain piece begins with patience and care. From shaping and glazing to hand-painting and firing, the process carries the quiet rhythm of traditional craft.

Shuli Studio hopes to create objects that are not only decorative, but also meaningful pieces that gently adorn everyday life.
