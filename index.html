About

Small objects with quiet details.

Shuli Studio is a personal handcraft studio focusing on handcrafted porcelain and delicate creations. Each piece is made in small quantities, carrying traces of handwork, time, and careful observation.

The studio celebrates slow making, soft colours, floral details, and objects that bring a gentle sense of beauty into daily life.

Story

From Jingdezhen to Auckland.

These pieces were all independently designed and produced by me in Jingdezhen, China. Known as the "Ceramic Capital of China," Jingdezhen boasts a long history of porcelain making and a rich tradition of handicrafts. My creative work began in this city.

Before coming to Auckland, I ran my own ceramic studio in Jingdezhen, handling every step from design and drawing to the final presentation myself. My work was also exhibited and sold through Taoxichuan, a renowned professional art ceramics market in Jingdezhen. This market, home to many ceramic artists, handcrafted brands, and contemporary artisans, gave me the opportunity to bring my work to a wider audience.

The preciousness of handmade objects lies in the fact that they encapsulate a period of the creator's life.

For me, each piece is not just an object, but a record of a handcrafted creative process. They embody my observations of the beauty of nature and daily life, preserving subtle, unique, and irreplaceable traces of the crafting process.

Now, I hope to bring Shuli Studio to Auckland, marking a new phase for the brand. The works currently on display are some of my creations sent from China to New Zealand. They are both a continuation of my past creative experiences and a starting point for a fresh start in this new environment.

Once everything is ready, I will gradually open my own ceramics studio in New Zealand, continuing to create more handmade ceramics and small handcrafted pieces. Shuli Studio hopes to start in Jingdezhen and slowly grow in Auckland, bringing quiet, delicate works with the warmth of handcrafted art into the daily lives of more people.

这些作品都由我在中国景德镇独立设计与制作。景德镇被称为“中国陶瓷之都”，这里拥有悠久的制瓷历史，也承载着深厚的手工艺传统。我的创作正是从这座城市开始的。

在来到奥克兰之前，我曾在景德镇经营自己的陶瓷工作室，从设计、绘制到作品呈现，每一个环节都是我独立亲自完成。我的作品也曾通过景德镇知名的专业艺术陶瓷市场“陶溪川”进行展示与销售。那里聚集了许多陶瓷艺术家、手作品牌与当代工艺创作者，也让我有机会将自己的作品带到更多真实的观众面前。

手作器物的珍贵之处，在于它凝结了创作者生命中的一段时间。

对我来说，每一件作品都不只是一个器物，而是一段手工创作过程的留存。它们承载着我对自然与日常生活美感的观察，也保留着手作过程中那些细微、独特而无法被完全复制的痕迹。

现在，我希望将 Shuli Studio 带到奥克兰，开启品牌新的阶段。目前展示的作品，是我从中国寄来新西兰的部分创作。它们既是过去创作经历的延续，也是我在新环境中重新开始的起点。

待一切准备就绪后，我将在新西兰逐步开设自己的陶瓷工作室，继续创作更多手工陶瓷与小型手作作品。Shuli Studio 希望从景德镇出发，在奥克兰慢慢生长，把安静、细腻、带有手作温度的作品带入更多人的日常生活。
